# layered-pco-bench results

Generated by `layered-pco-bench` (P7). decode = best of 5 runs, scalar_at = average over 1000 random indices.

## Datasets

- **synthetic**: the two columns carried over from P6 verbatim.
- **tpch_sf0p1_lineitem / tpch_sf0p1_orders**: TPC-H tables at scale
  factor 0.1, generated in-process by `tpchgen-arrow`. Lineitem has ~600k rows; orders has ~150k rows. Decimal128 columns are cast to i64 cents and date32 columns are widened to i64 so every variant sees the same `Primitive<I64>` input.
- **ClickBench / NYC taxi**: skipped (no in-tree loader, would need multi-GB downloads and a Parquet read path; budget on dataset acquisition was capped at 20 minutes per task spec).
